Your Questions, Answered

What is your venue's capacity?

Harvest House can accommodate up to 150 people indoors. We also have various stunning outdoor garden spaces suitable for different group sizes and event styles.

What kind of events do you host?

We are perfect for corporate functions, seminars, end-of-year events, and of course, beautiful weddings. Our versatile spaces and packages cater to a wide range of celebrations.

Is viewing by appointment only?

Yes, to ensure we can give you our undivided attention and show you everything Harvest House has to offer, viewing of the venue is by appointment only. Please contact us to schedule your visit.

Do you cater to dietary requirements like Halaal/Kosher?

Absolutely. For our Corporate/Office Functions (and often adaptable for other events), Halaal, Kosher, and Vegetarian options are available. Please discuss your specific needs with us.
